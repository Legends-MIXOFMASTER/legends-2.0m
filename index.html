Courses & Certification

Basics in Bartending

Course Overview

  • Introduction to Bartending & IBA Standards
  • Bar Tools & Glassware
  • Spirits, Liqueurs, and Mixers
  • Classic Cocktail Categories (IBA Official List)
  • Basic Techniques: Stir, Shake, Build, Muddle
  • Garnishes & Presentation
  • Responsible Service & Hygiene
  • Customer Interaction & Bar Etiquette

Sample IBA Cocktails

  • Martini: Gin, Dry Vermouth, Olive/Lemon Twist
  • Margarita: Tequila, Triple Sec, Lime Juice, Salt Rim
  • Negroni: Gin, Campari, Sweet Vermouth, Orange Garnish
  • Daiquiri: White Rum, Lime Juice, Sugar Syrup
  • Old Fashioned: Bourbon, Sugar, Bitters, Orange Peel
  • ...and more from the IBA Official List

Learning Outcomes

  • Understand key spirits and mixers
  • Identify and use basic bar tools
  • Prepare and serve classic cocktails to IBA standards
  • Apply proper hygiene and responsible service
